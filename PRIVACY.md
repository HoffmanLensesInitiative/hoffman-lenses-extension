# Hoffman Lenses — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## The short version

Hoffman Lenses collects nothing. Sends nothing. Stores nothing outside your browser. There is no server. There is no analytics. There is no telemetry. There is no advertising.

## The complete version

### What data Hoffman Lenses collects

None. Hoffman Lenses does not collect any personal data, browsing data, or usage data of any kind.

### What data Hoffman Lenses stores

Session statistics (posts scanned, flags detected, time on platform) are stored temporarily in your browser's memory for the duration of your browsing session. This data is cleared when you close the tab or browser. It is never written to persistent storage and never leaves your device.

### What network requests Hoffman Lenses makes

None. Hoffman Lenses makes zero network requests. It does not connect to any server, including hoffmanlenses.org, for any purpose. All processing happens entirely on your local device.

### What permissions Hoffman Lenses requires and why

**Access to facebook.com** (Phase 1): Required to read the visible content of Facebook pages in order to identify manipulation patterns. The extension reads page content locally — it does not transmit this content anywhere.

**Storage**: Required to maintain session statistics temporarily in browser memory during your current session.

**Alarms**: Required to update the session timer in the background.

### Third parties

Hoffman Lenses shares your data with no third parties because it collects no data to share.

### Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in any way that affects data collection, we will update this document and notify users through the extension update mechanism. Our commitment to collecting no data is permanent and will not change.
